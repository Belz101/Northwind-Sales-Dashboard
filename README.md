# Northwind-Sales-Dashboard

Description:
A single-page Power BI dashboard analysing the Northwind dataset. Shows KPIs, trends, top product categories, top-selling products, and key insights.

Data Source: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/jeetahirwar/northwind-traders

Visuals Included:
- KPI cards (Revenue, Quantity Sold, YoY, MoM)
- Trend line (Revenue over time)
- Revenue by Product Category chart
- Top 10 Products chart
- Insights Text Box

Key Insights:
- Top-performing category: Beverages, generating 35% of total revenue.
- Highest revenue month: December, indicating strong holiday sales.
- Top 3 products contributed 20% of total sales.
